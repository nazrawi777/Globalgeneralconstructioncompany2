✦ Our Story

Building with Integrity & Purpose

We turn your dreams into reality through quality construction and trusted delivery.

Global General Construction is a Grade-1 general construction company established to deliver high-quality infrastructure and building projects across Ethiopia. Since its founding, the company has focused on providing cost-effective, reliable, and timely construction services for both public and private clients.

Operating as a general contractor, the company specializes in road construction, building works, water supply and sewerage systems, and general civil infrastructure projects. Through consistent performance and strict adherence to standards, Global General Construction has earned recognition for quality workmanship, professional integrity, and reliability.

Every project we undertake is guided by safety, quality, and a commitment to meeting our clients’ expectations.

Co-founded by Engineer Kedir Hassen and supported by a team of qualified engineers, project managers, and skilled professionals, the company continues to expand its capacity. With strong technical expertise, modern equipment, and sound financial standing, Global General Construction remains dedicated to contributing to Ethiopia’s infrastructure development.

The company completed and delivered major infrastructure works, including water supply and sewerage systems, road upgrading projects, and restoration works for national facilities. These projects further strengthened trust with public-sector clients.
